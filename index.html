Filtru can automatically record your pre-infusion as soon as the coffee starts pouring.

Bluetooth scales

Get accurate results

Intuitive way to brew

App will guide you to pour water onto the ground coffee at an optimal rate. This ensures the best tasting coffee.

The interface turns red when you've not poured enough water

Real-time feedback

Record, review, and improve your pour over coffee over time with help from a coffee scale.

Hit the target

Filtru shows the target weight at step of the recipe and a red/yellow marker to indicate how close you are to hitting the mark.

Optimal Flow rate

The app records a flow-rate to help you get an even and consistent extraction.

Brew print of your coffee

For each coffee brewed with a Bluetooth scale Filtru keeps a graph of both pouring weight and flow rate.
